# 人物主图提示词模板

## 用途

供 `prompt-generation.md` 为人物主图任务生成英文提示词。

## 输入字段

- `asset_name`
- `state_name`
- `priority_level`
- `task_type`
- `source_evidence`
- `stable_face_identity`
- `visual_design_notes`
- `review_status`
- `review_reason`

## 主图模板

```text
[Character Name], [age]-year-old [woman/man/person], single-character bust portrait, plain clean neutral background, real portrait photograph, natural human realism.

Story identity: [social class / occupation / relationship role / story function].
Face identity: [face shape], [bone structure], [eye shape and spacing], [nose], [mouth], [jawline], [skin undertone], [age band], [core gaze quality].
Hair and grooming: [hair / grooming details].
Wardrobe: [story-supported clothing and material].
Expression and body language: [restrained expression], emotionally present, not posing for glamour.

Photography: eye-level camera, natural portrait lens, realistic head and shoulder proportions, raw portrait photography, subtle facial asymmetry, realistic pores, natural skin color variation, believable hairline and flyaways, fabric wrinkles.

Negative prompt: wrong age, generic attractive person, perfect symmetry, AI beauty face, influencer makeup, fashion model posing, commercial beauty lighting, plastic skin, waxy skin, over-retouched glamour, empty eyes, wide-angle distortion, unrealistic proportions, props, scene, other characters, readable text, watermark.

Image size: 9:16
```

## 约束

- 人物主图必须是单人。
- 背景必须是纯色或中性背景。
- 不写道具、场景、动作戏、关系戏。
- 中文证据不进入最终英文提示词正文。
